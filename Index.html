Miley Cyrus

American singer-songwriter and cultural icon

WHY

Why Miley is the best?

It’s unusual for an artist to be so multi-talented. Miley is a globally famous, acclaimed vocalist while also being an incredible dancer, actress and performer. Her concerts are well-known for their incredible spectacle - the sets, the choreography - and she sells out stadiums and arenas internationally. Miley has been at the peak of the music world for over 15 years and has always successfully reinvented herself, encompassing so many different genres like R&B, hip-hop, rock and country.

Additionally, Miley founded The Happy Hippie Foundation in 2015 to raise awareness about issues facing the LGBTQ+ community and address homelessness head-on. She has also used her platform to raise awareness and drive change within the Black Lives Matter movement, demonstrating that she is not only a talented artist but also an inspiring social activist.

See you again

"See You Again" is the debut single by American singer Miley Cyrus. It was released as the lead single from Meet Miley Cyrus (2007), the second soundtrack album from the Disney Channel original series Hannah Montana and the debut studio album of Cyrus. It was written by Cyrus (credited as Destiny Hope Cyrus) with the song's producers Antonina Armato and Tim James.

Endless Summer Vacation is the eighth studio album by American singer Miley Cyrus, released on March 10, 2023, through Columbia Records. It is her first body of original work since leaving RCA Records after her seventh studio album Plastic Hearts (2020) and signing with Columbia in early 2021.
